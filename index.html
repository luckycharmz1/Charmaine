    In May 2020, I graduated from Golden State Baptist College with an Associate's Degree in General Studies. Shortly after, I joined the mission field to assist missionaries Roberto and Joy Murillo. I served with them for about six months until their unfortunate passing. Following this, I returned to the United States and accepted the role of Digital Director at Victory Baptist Church in California. It was in this position that I deepened my knowledge of computers. While working diligently at VBC, I also attended Mission College, where I earned a Web Development Certificate to support the media ministry. After serving for over a year, I felt a call from Christ to return to teaching, which led me back to Mexico. I am now a high school teacher at Academia Americana El Camino, a ministry of Iglesia Bautista El Camino. I am continuing my education, aspiring to obtain degrees in both Computer Science and Education, with the goal of integrating STEM into my teaching. I created this website to help cover living expenses in Mexico, and 10% of the proceeds will be donated to the orphanage at Iglesia Bautista El Camino in Tijuana, Mexico. If you're interested in supporting this cause, please feel free to reach out to me.
